Ralph Records 10th Anniversary Radio Special

In February, 1982, Ralph Records contacted Mr. Penn Jillette, a nationally known entertainer and announcer, to see if he was interested in Ralph’s 10th Anniversary Radio Special. The format of the special, as specified in Mr. Jillette’s contract, was unusual, but the salary was sufficient, so Mr. Jillette agreed. At 8:00 AM on Monday, March 1, Mr. Jillette entered room 312 of the Bentley Motor Inn at 465 Grove Street (directly across the street from the Ralph Headquarters) in San Francisco.
